Who we are

AppAholic is built and operated by JustServicesPro Management and Consulting Ltd, a Nigerian technology and consulting company. We started AppAholic because too many small business tools are either bloated, overpriced, or built for markets that aren't ours.

Every app on AppAholic is designed with one question in mind: does this save someone real time or money? If not, we don't ship it.

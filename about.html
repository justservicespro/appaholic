About

Built for the way Nigerian businesses actually work.

AppAholic is a catalogued marketplace of practical software — not another generic app store, a stocked shelf of tools built for freelancers and small teams here.

Who we are

AppAholic started from a simple observation: most software is built for someone else's market. Nigerian freelancers and small businesses need tools priced and built for how they actually work — Naira pricing, WhatsApp-first workflows, offline-tolerant, no unnecessary complexity.

Every app on the shelf is either built in-house or by request — when enough people ask for the same thing, we build it and add it to the catalogue.

What makes us different

Naira-first pricing

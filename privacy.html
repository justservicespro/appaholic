Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how JustServicesPro Management and Consulting Ltd ("we", "us", "our"), the operator of AppAholic, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our marketplace.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you create an account or submit an app request
  • Payment information processed securely through Flutterwave (we do not store full card details)
  • Business details you provide when using apps like InvoiceKit (client names, invoice data)
  • Communications you send us, including support requests and app requests

We also automatically collect limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, to keep the platform secure and improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process purchases and deliver app downloads
  • To send transactional emails (purchase receipts, request status updates, password resets)
  • To respond to support requests and app build requests
  • To improve our apps and marketplace based on usage patterns

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Email Communications

By creating an account or submitting a request, you agree to receive transactional emails related to your account, purchases, and requests. You may opt out of promotional emails at any time from your account notification settings. Transactional emails (receipts, security alerts) cannot be disabled while your account is active.

4. Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Flutterwave, a licensed payment service provider. We do not store your card number, CVV, or banking PIN on our servers. Please refer to Flutterwave's own privacy policy for details on how they handle payment data.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Flutterwave, to process payments
  • Email delivery providers, to send transactional emails
  • Law enforcement, only when legally required

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and transaction data for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with tax and accounting obligations under Nigerian law.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing info@justservices.pro. We will respond within 14 business days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

AppAholic is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice on the platform.

11. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at info@justservices.pro.
